# Voice & Tone

The otma brand voice guides how we communicate with our audience — from marketing copy to in-app messages.

## Brand Personality

- **Clear** — We explain things simply, without unnecessary jargon.
- **Confident** — We speak with authority while remaining approachable.
- **Human** — We write like real people talking to real people.
- **Forward-thinking** — We focus on what's possible, not limitations.

## Tone Guidelines

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Marketing | Inspiring, energetic | "Build something you're proud of." |
| Onboarding | Friendly, encouraging | "You're all set — let's get started." |
| Error messages | Calm, helpful | "Something went wrong. Try again or contact support." |
| Documentation | Precise, neutral | "Run `npm install` to install dependencies." |

## Writing Rules

1. **Use active voice.** "We updated your settings" not "Your settings were updated."
2. **Be concise.** Cut every word that doesn't add meaning.
3. **Avoid idioms** that may not translate across cultures.
4. **Never use ALL CAPS** for emphasis — use bold instead.
5. **Contractions are fine** — they make copy feel more natural.
